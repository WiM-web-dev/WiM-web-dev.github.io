Women in Mathematics at Berkeley (WIM) is an organization that serves to foster a community and provide a network amongst the undergraduate women in mathematics at Cal. WIMMERS hope to establish a long-lasting relationship with other Berkeley undergraduates, graduate students, and professors, while finding unity in their common love of math.

We have weekly meetings on Mondays from 6-8 P.M. in 1015 Evans, with activities ranging from study hours to talks from graduate students to game nights. We also do field trips to the Exploratorium in San Francisco and other math-related activities outside of our Monday meetings. Check out our events page and calendar for updates!

Founded in February 2014 by Katrina Passarella and Meaghan McConlogue, WIM is still a fairly new student organization but has grown and accomplished a remarkable amount within the last year. Check out our pages to see what we’re all about!
